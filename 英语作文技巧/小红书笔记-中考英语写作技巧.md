## 🔥 一、写作"四步法"——下笔前先走完这套流程

很多同学一拿到题目就写，写到一半发现跑题了...

**记住四个字：审 → 列 → 连 → 改**

| 步骤 | 核心要点 |
|------|---------|
| **①审** | 看清题目要求、文体、人称、时态（日记/故事→过去时；介绍类→现在时） |
| **②列** | 列提纲！不会写的词用同义词替换，别硬写没把握的 |
| **③连** | 连句成文，一般分3段，长短句搭配，简单句+复合句交替 |
| **④改** | 检查拼写、时态、单复数、主谓一致、标点 |

**💡 小口诀：三审（体裁/时态/人称）+ 三思（词汇→短语→句式）+ 三查（要点/拼写语法/连贯）**

## 🎯 二、万能三段式——任何作文都逃不出这个结构

```
第1段（2句）→ 引入主题，亮出观点
第2段（3-6句）→ 正文展开，2-3个论点 + 过渡连接词
第3段（2句）→ 总结升华，紧扣主题
```

## 🏆 三、中考必背10大万能句型（背就完了！）

### 开头句
1️⃣ **It goes without saying that...** 不用说……
> 例：It goes without saying that it pays to keep early hours.（不用说，早睡早起是值得的。）

2️⃣ **Among various kinds of...** 在各种……之中
> 例：Among various kinds of sports, I like jogging in particular.

3️⃣ **In my opinion / As far as I am concerned...** 就我而言……

4️⃣ **With the development of...** 随着……的发展
> 例：With the rapid development of technology, our life has changed a lot.

### 过渡句
5️⃣ **There are many reasons for this, but in general, they come down to three major ones.** （承上启下的万能过渡）

6️⃣ **Every coin has two sides.** 凡事都有两面性

### 高级表达
7️⃣ **not only...but also...** 不仅……而且……
> 例：Not only can she play well, but she also writes music.（倒装更高级！）

8️⃣ **so...that...** 如此……以至于……
> 例：I was so upset that I wanted to give up.

9️⃣ **Whenever I..., I cannot but feel...** 每当……我就忍不住……
> 例：Whenever I think of the clear brook near my home, I cannot but feel sad.

🔟 **It is + adj. + for sb. to do sth.** 对某人来说做某事是……的
> 例：It is important for us to protect the environment.

## 📚 四、按题型套模板——考场上直接默写！

### 📖 议论文模板（最常考！）

**正反观点型：**
- 第1段：Recently we've had a discussion about whether... Our opinions are divided.
- 第2段：Most students are in favour of it. Here are the reasons. First... Second... Finally...
- 第3段：However, the others are strongly against it. Their reasons are as follows...
- 第4段：Personally speaking, the advantages overweigh the disadvantages, so I support it.

**观点论述型（总-分-总）：**
- 第1段：As a student, I am strongly in favour of the decision. The reasons may be listed as follows.
- 第2段：First of all... Secondly... Besides...
- 第3段：In conclusion, I believe that...

**How to 类：**
- 第1段：提出现象/困难
- 第2段：Many ways can help to solve this problem. First of all... Another way... Finally...
- 第3段：These are not the best but the most effective measures we can take.

### ✉️ 书信模板

**开头：**
- How nice to hear from you again.
- I'm writing to thank you for your help during my stay in...

**结尾：**
- I'm looking forward to your reply.
- With best wishes.

### 📢 口头通知模板

- 开头：Ladies and gentlemen, may I have your attention, please? I have an announcement to make.
- 正文：All the students are required to attend... / Please take your notebooks and...
- 结尾：That's all. Thank you.

## 🔗 五、连接词大全——让作文变"丝滑"

| 功能 | 词汇 |
|------|------|
| **表顺序** | first, to begin with, what's more, finally, last but not least |
| **表转折** | however, nevertheless, on the other hand, whereas |
| **表递进** | in addition, besides, moreover, furthermore |
| **表因果** | therefore, thus, as a result, consequently |
| **表举例** | for example, for instance, such as, take...as an example |
| **表总结** | in a word, in conclusion, to sum up, in brief |
| **表个人观点** | as far as I am concerned, from my point of view, personally speaking |

## ✨ 六、高分加分技巧（90%的人不知道）

### 1️⃣ 字数不够？"数字论证法"来凑！
> According to a recent survey, about 78.9% of the students...

### 2️⃣ 开头用名言（没有就"编"！）
> A proverb says, "You are only young once."
> As an old saying goes, "Every coin has two sides."

### 3️⃣ 能用高级词就别用简单词
| 普通表达 | 高级替换 |
|----------|---------|
| think | believe / hold the view that / maintain |
| very | extremely / remarkably |
| important | essential / significant / indispensable |
| more and more | an increasing number of / a growing number of |
| good | beneficial / favorable / positive |
| bad | harmful / negative / detrimental |

### 4️⃣ 倒装句是加分利器
- Only in this way can we solve the problem properly.
- Not only does it affect our health, but it also causes pollution.

### 5️⃣ 强调句用起来
- It is joining the basketball club that brings me a lot of happiness.
- It was Jack who encouraged me when I wanted to give up.

### 6️⃣ 三段式结尾一定要升华
> 普通：I hope I can learn English well.
> 升华：As long as we keep practicing, I'm confident that a bright future is awaiting us.
