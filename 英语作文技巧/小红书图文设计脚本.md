## 图5：十大万能句型（下） + 高级替换词

**标题：🏆 十大万能句型（下）+ 高级替换词**

**句型 6-10：**
```
6️⃣ not only... but also... （倒装更高级！）
   → Not only can she play well, but she also writes music.

7️⃣ so...that...
   → I was so upset that I wanted to give up.

8️⃣ Whenever I..., I cannot but feel...
   → 万能情感表达句！

9️⃣ It is + adj. + for sb. to do sth.

🔟 There is no denying that...
```

**底部重点框（红底白字）：**
```
⚠️ 阅卷老师最讨厌的单词替换表：
think → believe/maintain/hold the view that
very → extremely/remarkably
more and more → an increasing number of
good → beneficial/positive
bad → harmful/detrimental
important → essential/significant/indispensable
```

## 图6：议论文模板

**标题：📖 议论文万能模板（最常考！拿来直接套）**

**四个模板分栏：**

**📌 正反观点型**
```
P1: Recently we've had a discussion about whether...
    Our opinions are divided.
P2: Most students are in favour of it. Here are the reasons.
    First... Second... Finally...
P3: However, the others are strongly against it.
    Their reasons are as follows...
P4: Personally speaking, the advantages overweigh the disadvantages.
```

**📌 观点论述型（总-分-总）**
```
P1: As a student, I am strongly in favour of...
    The reasons may be listed as follows.
P2: First of all... Secondly... Besides...
P3: In conclusion, I believe that...
```

**📌 How to 类**
```
P1: 提出困难/现象
P2: Many ways can help to solve this problem.
    First of all... Another way... Finally...
P3: These are not the best but the only measures...
```

**📌 利弊分析型**
```
P1: Nowadays, there is a widespread concern over...
P2: Advantages: Firstly... Secondly...
P3: Disadvantages: To begin with... In addition...
P4: To sum up, we should try to bring the advantages into full play.
```

## 图7：书信 & 口头通知模板

**标题：✉️ 书信 + 📢 口头通知 —— 格式分绝对不能丢！**

**左半部分：✉️ 书信**
```
【开头】
• How nice to hear from you again.
• I'm glad to receive your letter of...
• I'm writing to thank you for...

【正文过渡】
• Let me tell you something about...
• I'd like to introduce...to you.

【结尾】
• I'm looking forward to your reply.
• I'd appreciate it if you could reply earlier.
• With best wishes.
```

**右半部分：📢 口头通知**
```
【呼语】
Ladies and gentlemen, may I have your
attention, please? I have an announcement
to make.

【正文】
• All the teachers and students are
  required to attend it.
• Please take your notebooks and make notes.

【结束语】
• Everybody is welcome to attend it.
• That's all. Thank you.
```

**底部提示：**
```
⚠️ 书信格式：称呼→正文→结束语→签名
缺少任何一个都会扣分！
```

## 图8：连接词大全 + 加分技巧

**标题：🔗 连接词大全——让作文"丝滑起来"**

**左侧表格：**
| 功能 | 词汇 |
|------|------|
| 顺序 | first, to begin with, what's more, finally, last but not least |
| 转折 | however, nevertheless, on the other hand, whereas |
| 递进 | in addition, besides, moreover, furthermore |
| 因果 | therefore, thus, as a result, consequently |
| 举例 | for example, for instance, such as, take...as an example |
| 总结 | in a word, in conclusion, to sum up, in brief |

**右侧加分技巧：**
```
✨ 加分大招：
① 倒装句：
Only in this way can we solve the problem.

② 强调句：
It is...that...

③ 名言开头：
As a proverb says,...

④ 数字论证：
According to a recent survey, 85% of...

⑤ 结尾升华：
I'm confident that a bright future
is awaiting us because...
```
